Recent News

Luka Doncic Traded
On February 2nd, Luka Doncic was traded from the Dallas Mavericks to the LA Lakers for Anthony Davis. The trade was outright confusing and it was met with criticism from many fans. Doncic is a one of a kind player, and nobody expected him to ever be traded. Even though Anthony Davis is an incredible player, he is way older than Doncic and a lot worse. Not only did this trade possibly end the Dallas Maverick's championship hopes for the season, but it has also completely killed fan morale and may have possibly killed the Dallas Mavericks as a franchise.


Wembanyama gets his star teammate
On the same day as the Luka trade, De'Aaron Fox was apart of a massive 3-team that sent him to the San Antonio Spurs, Zach Lavine to the Sacramento Kings, and numerous pieces to the Chicago Bulls. This trade pairs up Fox and the absolute savant, Victor Wembanyama, making the Spurs a serious threat in the west. Kings fans were somewhat confused as to why Fox was traded so suddenly, and for a player in Lavine that really does not help them much. An interesting trade for all teams involved that will definitely shake things up.


LA Clippers dump PJ Tucker
Not every trade can be a blockbuster that sends shockwaves throughout the league. On February 1st, the LA Clippers were finally able to get rid of PJ Tucker. Tucker has been making a whopping 10 million dollars a year to just sit on the bench. Tucker was once a very solid basketball player that any team would have loved to acquire, but as the years go on, Tucker is simply not good enough to stay on the court anymore. The Clippers free up some space to possibly make moves in the buyout marker.
